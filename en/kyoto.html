Kyoto

Live and study in the beautiful and historical city of Kyoto

Kyoto, also known as the ancient capital, served as the Imperial capital of Japan for more than one thousand years. To this day, numerous cultural assets are well preserved and stil remain throughout the city. You can also experience the infamous Japanese tea ceremony and flower arrangement in Kyoto. In addition to their beautiful temples and nature, Kyoto is also home to several top universities, for those of you planning to do further studies.
