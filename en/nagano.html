Nagano
長野

Live and study at the vibrant castle town Nagano

Nagano has a lot of grand, natural and beautiful sceneries surrounding its area, making it the perfect area to study. Nagano hosted the 1998 Winter Olympics Games, and is just a little bit over one hour by Shinkansen from Tokyo. There are also a lot of ski resorts near Nagano for those of you who would like to try winter sports during winter. A ten minute bus ride from JR Nagano Station will take you to Zenkoji Temple, one of the most important and popular temple in Japan.
