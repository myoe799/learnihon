Live and study in the heart of Japan

Formerly known as Edo, Tokyo has served as Japan's capital since Meiji Restoration in the 1860s and has since then evolved into one of the largest and most vibrant metropolis in the world. Despite being a large metropolitan with numerous skyscrapers, Tokyo also has numerous green spaces for when you need to take a break and enjoy nature. Tokyo offers unlimited options for entertainments, foods, and plenty of chances for you to experience Japanese culture while you improve your Japanese skills!
