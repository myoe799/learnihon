ISI
International Study Institute

Kyoto Campus


ISI Kyoto campus was just opened in July 2017 and is the newest campus in ISI group. If you are interested in going to a university in Japan, ISI might have just what you need. Offering rich university preparatory courses, you will be prepared to ace Japanese university entrance examinations! ISI also offers other different courses, such as college pathway class for students interested in enrolling in a vocational school and JLPT preparation class for students who are aiming to ace JLPT. ISI also provide Job hunting support to students who are interested in starting a career in Japan.

ABOUT THE SCHOOL


Academic Japanese Course

If you are looking to do higher education in Japan, ISI offers a range of academic Japanese courses to help you get into Japanese universities/graduate school. This includes preparations for EJU, JLPT and university entrance examinations.


Monthly Enrolment

Monthly enrolment is available at ISI Kyoto Campus for any level, even if you are a complete beginner and have never studied Japanese before, so you can start learning as soon as possible.


Experience Japanese Culture

There are a lot of well preserved temples and traditional architectures in Kyoto. You can feel the historical atmosphere as you take a stroll around the city. ISI Kyoto campus also offers a cultural experience class elective, where students can deepen their understanding of Japanese culture. During lessons, students can also experience traditional Japanese culture such as Japanese tea ceremony, zen meditation, and many more!


Small Class Size

With a maximum of 15 students per class, students are encouraged to participate in class activies. Teachers can also know the students better and students can study in a relaxed atmosphere.


Convenient Location

ISI Kyoto campus is located 7 minutes walking distance away from Enmachi station on JR Sagano-Sanin line.

COURSE INFORMATION


General Japanese Course

This course is designed for students interested in enhancing their overal Japanese skills. Students will be able to reach a level where they can pass N3 JLPT exam while also enjoying elective classes. Afterwards, students who wish to continue their study can transfer to the academic Japanese course.

Academic Japanese Course

The academic Japanese Course is designed for students who are interested in pursuing higher education in Japan. This course aims to develop students' Japanese language skills in order to pass Japanese university or college entrance examination.

Elective Classes

Cultural Experience Class
Students can deepen their understanding of Japanese traditional culture, such as Japanese tea ceremony and zen meditation.
JLPT Prep. Class
Students will be prepared to do well in JLPT N1 and N2 level examination.
Practical Conversation Class
Students will develop their conversational skills through various class activities, such as voice-over acting in Japanese animation.
Reading and Writing Comprehension Class
Students will read and write about their experience on Japanese culture to enhance their reading/writing skills.
University Pathway Class
Students will be prepared to ace EJU (Examination for Japanese University for International Students) and JLPT examinations.
College Pathway Class
Students will be able to choose elective classes on top of core classes. The school also organises extracurricular activities to deepen students' understanding on Japanese culture.
