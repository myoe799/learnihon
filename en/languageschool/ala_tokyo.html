ALA
Academy of Language Arts


ALA Japanese Language School offers a homey study environment and personalised hospitality for their students, which has attracted students from over 30 countries around the world. ALA also provides a wide range of supports for their students, such as higher education preparation and assistance for students who wish to start a career in Japan. Other than Japanese language, students are also given a chance to learn about Japanese society, culture and customs through ALA's school events and extracurricular activity.

ABOUT THE SCHOOL


Conversation Focused Classes

ALA provides practical spoken Japanese that helps equip their student face various situations. This includes survival Japanese phrases, business Japanese, academic Japanese, and many more.


Low Teacher-to-Student Ratio

ALA offers a small and focused class size, with an average of 12 students per class. This allows teachers to personally know their student's strengths and weaknesses, and also encourages students to be more active in class.


International Environment

ALA has had students from over 30 countries, and the common language used for students to converse with each other is of course, Japanese.


Full Support

ALA goes the extra mile in supporting their students. They provide assistance to students who would like to find a part-time and/or full time job, and consultations for students who would like to continue to higher education.


Convenient Location

ALA is only three minutes walk from Iidabashi Station, located in the center of Tokyo, and is accessible from various train lines: JR Sobu Chuo Line, three Tokyo Metro Lines, and Toei Oedo Line.
Iidabashi is very close to major stations: 7 minutes to Akihabara, 9 minutes to Ikebukuro and 10 minutes to Shinjuku by train.

COURSE INFORMATION


General Japanese Course

Focuses on Japanese conversation ability. Also aims to improve students' reading and writing composition comprehension. Students can immediately apply what they learn in class directly in real life situation

Academic Japanese Course

Well suited for students aiming to take Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT) and students planning on applying to Japanese universities and graduate school. Students will study all aspects of Japanese(speaking, listening, reading and writing) in a well-balanced manner.


Course Duration

Class Level Breakdown

Your level is determined through a level check interview with one of the school's teacher
LEVEL GENERAL AIM REQUIRED DURATION
B1 Able to understand and engage in daily conversation 3 months
B2 Able to understand basic instruction and take part in basic conversation on a given topic 6 months
I1 Able to express simple opinions in a familiar context 9 months
I2 Able to communicate properly and exchange information and opinions on a given context 12 months
I3 Able to communicate relatively fluently on various topics including daily life situation and one's interest 15 months
A1 Able to communicate appropriately and nearly fluently with regards to one's interest 18 months
A2 Able to communicate fluently and naturally with regards to areas of one's interest 21 months
A3 Able to communicate effectively, fluently and naturally on subjects of general interest 24 months
A4 Able to communicate effectively, fluently and naturally in any given topic Above 24 months
