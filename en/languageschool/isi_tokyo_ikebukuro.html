ISI
International Study Institute

Tokyo / Ikebukuro Campus


If you are interested in going to a university in Japan, ISI might have just what you need. Offering rich university preparatory courses, you will be prepared to ace Japanese university entrance examinations! ISI also offers other different courses, such as college pathway class for students interested in enrolling in a vocational school and JLPT preparation class for students who are aiming to ace JLPT. ISI also provide Job hunting support to students who are interested in starting a career in Japan.

ABOUT THE SCHOOL


Academic Japanese Courses

If you are looking to do higher education in Japan, ISI offers a range of academic Japanese courses to help you get into Japanese universities/graduate school. This includes preparations for EJU, JLPT and university entrance examinations.


Job Hunting Support

ISI offers a job hunting class where students will learn business Japanese, Japanese business manners, one-on-one interview practices. Outside of class hours, students are offered personal career counseling sessions. The school also helps students in finding part-time jobs and give them tips and advices on how to find a job in Japan.


Interaction with Japanese Students

The campus features a vocational school, so interactions with Japanese students is a possibility. The school organises events throughout the year, participate in community volunteering events, and other extracurricular activities, so you will have plenty of chances to interact with Japanese students.


Library

If you need extra materials for your studies, the school offers a wide range of reference materials in the school library.


Convenient Location

ISI Ikebukuro campus is located around 5 minutes walking distance from Ikebukuro station, which has 8 transportation lines. Not far from the stations are various department stores, restaurants and a wide range of shops. Ikebukuro is also less than 15 minutes away from major stations like Shibuya or Shinjuku by train.



COURSE INFORMATION


Standard Japanese Course

To meet various students' needs, ISI offers a wide range of programs. In the standard Japanese course, students will focus on all aspects of Japanese language (reading, listening, writing and speaking). Once students reach the intermediate and advanced level, students will be given the options to take elective classes offered by the school: University Pathway Class, College Pathway Class, JLPT Preparation Class, Practical Conversation Class and Business Japanese Class.

Elective Classes

University Pathway Class
Students will be prepared to ace EJU (Examination for Japanese University for International Students) and JLPT examinations.
College Pathway Class
Students will be able to choose elective classes on top of core classes. The school also organises extracurricular activities to deepen students' understanding on Japanese culture.
JLPT Prep. Class
Students will be prepared to do well in JLPT N1 and N2 level examination.
Practical Conversation Class
Students will develop their conversational skills through various class activities, such as voice-over acting in Japanese animation.
Business Japanese Class
Students will learn business Japanese and Japanese business manners. Career counselling sessions are also offered in this class.
